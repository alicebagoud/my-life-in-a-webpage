The places I have lived 📍

Tournus

Tournus is the village I was born in. It has a big river called La Saône, a beautiful Cathedral and great sunsets.
It is located in one a the best wine regions called La Bourgogne.

Australia

Australia is an incredible country. I has so many beautiful views and people are really nice.
I lived in Brisbane and Melbourne and also visited Sydney.

Colombia

It was really hard to choose a picture for this one. I have so many and this country is sooo beautiful.
Guatapé is a little town in Antioquia near Medellin.
It has many many colors and a big big stone you can climb and walk up. It has many little islands you can see from the top of the stone. I visited it in March, this year.
I really recommend this country to visit, it is for me the best country in the world hahaha.

Lyon

The best city in France. I lived there 7 years and don't regret one single moment. It is an incredible city for kids, students, adults and in all kind of seasons, The Alpes in winter and many places and lakes in summer such as Annecy or Aiguebelette.
